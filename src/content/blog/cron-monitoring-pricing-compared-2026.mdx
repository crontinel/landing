Choosing a cron monitor in 2026 means reading past headline prices. Better Stack **removed** cron monitoring entirely. Remaining tools split into **per-monitor** billing and **per-app / flat** tiers.

This page compares what Laravel teams actually pay for scheduler visibility, plus when queue and Horizon depth matter more than ping checks.

List prices change; use each vendor's pricing page before purchase. Crontinel's public tiers are on [pricing](/pricing/).

## Crontinel

- **Free:** 1 app, limited history, enough to validate integration
- **Pro ($19/mo):** Multiple apps, longer retention, Slack/email/PagerDuty/webhook
- **Team ($49/mo):** Unlimited apps, team seats, custom rules
- **OSS:** `composer require crontinel/laravel` — no SaaS fee if you self-host the dashboard

**Best for:** Laravel apps with Horizon, teams that want one predictable bill without counting monitors.

## Cronitor

Heartbeat and schedule monitoring priced by **monitor count**. Strong for polyglot cron (shell scripts, non-Laravel jobs).

**Best for:** Simple dead-man pings across many small jobs when you do not need queue internals.

**Gap for Laravel:** No Horizon supervisor or queue depth without custom work.

## CronRadar

Low per-monitor positioning (~$1/monitor in marketing). Cost rises as auto-discovery registers more scheduled tasks.

**Best for:** Greenfield apps with few tasks and multi-framework cron (Laravel + Python sidecars).

See [Crontinel vs CronRadar](/vs/crontinel-vs-cronradar/) for feature depth.

## Better Stack and thenping.me

Do not budget for Better Stack cron in 2026; migrate if you still have heartbeat URLs ([migration guide](/blog/migrate-from-better-stack-cron-monitoring/)). thenping.me is closed; Crontinel published a [migration path](/blog/migrate-from-thenping-me/) for affected users.

## Hidden costs to model

| Cost type | Per-monitor tools | Crontinel-style |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Adding 10 new scheduled tasks | Often +10 monitors | Usually same app tier |
| Horizon / queue alerts | Extra vendor or DIY | Included in Laravel package |
| Staging + production | 2× monitors each env | 2 apps on same plan |
| Compliance / air-gap | Rare | Self-host OSS |

## Recommendation by team size

- **Solo, 1 Laravel app:** Crontinel free or CronRadar low monitor count — pick Horizon depth vs lowest line item
- **Small SaaS (Horizon):** Crontinel Pro; avoid ping-only tools unless queues are trivial
- **Agency, many small crons:** Cronitor or CronRadar per-monitor can win on shell-only workloads
- **Regulated / self-host:** Crontinel OSS only
